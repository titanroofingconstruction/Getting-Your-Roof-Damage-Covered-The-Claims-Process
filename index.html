Getting Your Roof Damage Covered: The Claims Process

Roof damage can be a profoundly stressful experience for homeowners. Aside from the damage to your sanctuary, navigating the insurance claim process can be a dizzying experience. At Titan Roofing, we want to take the stress and uncertainty out of the process and help you get your roof covered quickly and efficiently. Read on for a step-by-step overview of the process.

Understand Your Coverage

First and foremost, it's important to understand what your insurance policy covers. Most homeowner's insurance policies cover roof damage caused by sudden events, such as severe weather conditions or falling objects. However, roof damage caused by wear and tear or lack of maintenance is usually not covered.

When it comes to filing a claim, it's crucial to act quickly. Don't wait until the roof damage gets worse or the insurance company might deny your claim. As soon as you notice any damage, contact your insurance company and report it. They will assign an adjuster to assess the damage and determine the amount of compensation you're entitled to.

Document the Roof Damage

It's also important to document the damage. Take photos and videos of the issue, and keep receipts of any repairs you've made. This will help your insurance company determine the extent of the damage and the cost of repairs.

Once the adjuster has assessed the roof damage, they will provide you with a claim estimate. This estimate will include the cost of repairs or replacement, as well as the amount of your deductible. Your deductible is the amount you'll need to pay out of pocket before your insurance company covers the rest.
